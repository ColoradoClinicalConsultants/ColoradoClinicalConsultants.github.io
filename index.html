About Us

Colorado Clinical Consultants provides outpatient behavioral health care and consulting services across Colorado. Direct services include individual and group therapy, clinical assessments (level-of-care, diagnostic, and risk assessments), case management, and peer coaching for adolescents, individuals, and families managing mental health and substance use needs.

We also provide consulting services to behavioral health agencies, treatment programs, schools, teams, and coaching organizations.

Our Team
